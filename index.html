DeVante Parker

Biography

Growing up I spent my early years living in Dallas, Texas. For majority of those years I was raised by my step-father who was a discharged veteran of the Army. This taught me to pay attention to things that most people wouldn't think would be necessary to note. As I got older, I learned to become very proficient with noticing details and patterns that helped me when it mattered, especially when I played basketball and soccer. Right before middle school, we moved to Oklahoma to be around family. During my senior year of high school, I was offered a scholarship to play soccer at the University Of Southern Nazarene. I played there for two years before moving my focus to academics by attending Bryan University in Tempe, Arizona to finish my Bachelor's degree in Science. Later, in 2022, I found my true passion and decided to join a 24-week bootcamp to learn how to help design, create, and update new software with earning my certificate in Software Development from DevCode.

Work Experience

After graduating from college, I immediately starting working as a personal trainer and running my own business as a contract trainer working out of a nearby gym facility. I trained for almost 7 years as a part-time trainer and one year as a full-time trainer before moving into the insurance industry as a sales agent. I thought since the money would be promising that once I get my license as a insurance agent I would be set but, no one told me about the cold-calls, the long unpaid hours because it was full commission, the door-to-door footwork, and the amount of stress that is put upon you to sell. I immediately looked for better ways to use my license with different companies until I was able to find a work-from-home tele-sales company that sold medicare. The training was long and boring but it was consistent hourly wages that was more than I had ever made. I stuck around through that position until I could get out of the sales department and work more on the administrative side. Now, I have been working as a Broker Service Representative for the last two years and during that time, I was educated on what Software Development was all about. I was referred by a co-worker at the time to look into the website Udemy and try a course he recommended. Once I completed my first course, I fell in love with the database, the analytics, and the meticulous depth of the work. So I bought more courses and decided that this is where I want to be! However, I needed a faster and better structured way of breaking into the software industry.
